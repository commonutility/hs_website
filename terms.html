Terms & Conditions

24 April 2021 Our mission is to bring campuses together through authentic conversations and discourse about issues in our communities. Librex is moderated by community reporting and volunteer student moderators. We moderate Librex posts in accordance with our rules: Be legal. Keep Librex free of hate speech and harassment. Engage in illicit activities elsewhere. Be anonymous. Respect identities and privacy of individuals. Share personally identifiable information only when it already exists publicly (e.g., on social media or in the news). Be respectful. Uphold the community and the integrity of discourse. Write posts with titles descriptive of their contents and use appropriate tags. Refrain from posting intrusive spam and off-topic replies. Be mature. Include a handwritten "Librex" verification within adult (NSFW) images containing identifiable individuals. You can help us by reporting content for review. We trust our community to help make Librex a constructive venue for free and productive speech. Happy Librexing!
