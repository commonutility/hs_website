Content Policy

We made circles.co anonymous so people could openly talk about the topics they care about.

At the same time, content is also monitored via the moderating team and user reports. Users can report content by clicking on the  “...”  icon accompanying a post, comment, or reply.

We hope users agree to follow these rules. Violation of these rules could result in a lifetime ban.


1. Stay Anonymous

    Do not post personally identifiable information (names, pictures, social media, etc.) related to 

    another classmate.


2. Respect other users

    No hate speech (attacks on the basis of race, gender). No threats. No 

    harassment of specific individuals or users.


3. Maturity

    Please make sure to responsibly label posts that may considered "not safe for school" with a "mature" tag. Refrain from posting explicit content. Gore, nudity,  

    is prohibited.


4. Quality Content

    Make sure to appropriately title posts and text. Do not create spam.


In general, recognize that circles.co is meant to be a community for genuine interaction. Don’t ruin it for the rest of us.

Thanks for reading :)
