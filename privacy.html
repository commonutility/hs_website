Privacy Policy


Circles.co was made anonymous so people could feel open about their interactions. The team values personal privacy above all, which is why sensitive information such as email addresses are stored separately from a user’s login information.

The purpose of this page is to describe the collection and usage of data for the circles.co app and website. 

By using circles.co, you consent to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. We use the data to improve circles.co.

Your Information

Circles.co collects some information about your account and content to make for a functional experience and to ensure that you go to the school that you attend the school associated with the email that you use to sign up. Here is how we interact with your information:

  • Sign Up Info: At sign up, you will be assigned a user ID. 

  • User Generated Contact: We may collect information as you interact with our app to improve your experience. Specifically, we collect the text of your posts and comments as well as voting behavior and bookmarks. All posts and comments are public and are publicly displayed. We reserve the right to view, share and modify posts from our users. Be careful not to post any identifying information in your content. 

  • Additional Info: We may collect information you send to us through a form or communication with our company. This includes communications through third-party social media sites, requests for customer support, or any other method of communication with circles.co or our employees and partners.

Information Collection

Below is some of the other information we may collect as you interact with circles.co:

  • Usage Information: We may collect statistics on the frequency with which you access circles.co and the posts you view. We may also look at your interactions with posts. This information is used to make for a better user experience and to tweak the app and website.

  • Website statistics tracking: We collect statistics on our app and website using Google Analytics.

Information Sharing

By using circles.co you consent to share the content of your posts and comments with the public.

Your posts may also be used to comply with legal regulations and processes.

Aggregate data may also be shared for marketing and technical analysis.

Your Posts

Your posts remain public so long as we do not delete them from circles.co.

We may keep archived copies of some information for legal reasons.

Your posts will remain anonymous unless we perceive them as a legal threat to another person.
