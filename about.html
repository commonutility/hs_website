About Circles

Circles.co is an app made by other students who want to see high schoolers talk openly about the topics they really care about.

We made the app anonymous so conversation about classes, sports, memes, drama, events, anything, can happen genuinely.

On the app you can make posts, send dms, send memes, put out a survey, and more.

We’ll be launching soon at a highschool near you. You can download from the app store, or if you’re already a user, send us feedback through the settings icon in your profile page. Thanks for reading :)
